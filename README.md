## Overview

This repository documents the work carried out during the Practice School II (PS-1102) internship at **Toposel Digital Marketing Pvt. Ltd.** — a performance-driven digital marketing agency working with brands in fitness, wellness, e-commerce, and lifestyle.

The internship focused on the **design, configuration, and deployment of automated retention marketing campaigns** across multiple communication channels including WhatsApp, email, and social media platforms.

The role combined creative asset development with technical automation configuration — spanning HTML email templates, WhatsApp Business API integration, trigger-based workflow setup, user segmentation logic, UTM tracking, and campaign performance monitoring.

---

## Role

**Retention and Graphic Design Intern**

Core responsibilities included:

- Designing email, WhatsApp, and social media creatives for campaign deployment
- Structuring and editing HTML email templates with embedded UTM tracking links
- Registering and configuring WhatsApp messaging templates on Meta Business Suite
- Configuring trigger-based automation workflows on Customer.io and Interakt
- Implementing user segmentation logic based on behavioural parameters
- Setting up time-based scheduling and conditional campaign branching
- Conducting test runs and validating deployments before going live
- Monitoring delivery logs, debugging failures, and resolving template mismatches
- Maintaining structured Excel-based tracking sheets with campaign metadata and performance data

## Campaign Lifecycle

Every campaign executed during the internship followed a structured lifecycle:

1. **Requirement Gathering** — Define campaign objective, target segment, and communication channel
2. **Creative Development** — Design email/WhatsApp/social media assets aligned with campaign goals
3. **Template Configuration** — Structure HTML templates or register WhatsApp templates on Meta Business Suite
4. **Automation Setup** — Configure trigger logic, segmentation rules, and scheduling on automation platforms
5. **Testing & Validation** — Conduct test deployments, verify template mapping, and review delivery logs
6. **Live Deployment** — Launch campaign after final validation
7. **Performance Tracking** — Monitor delivery rates, click-through metrics, and engagement data via UTM parameters and Meta Analytics
8. **Feedback & Optimisation** — Implement revisions based on performance data and stakeholder feedback
